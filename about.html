About Us

Innovating with passion, delivering with precision

Our Story

Welcome to Maikarfi Metal Construction Ltd, a premier provider of innovative engineering and fabrication solutions dedicated to transforming ideas into reality. With a strong foundation built on technical expertise and customer-centric values, we are proud to serve diverse industries, delivering exceptional quality and sustainable solutions.

Founded with a commitment to excellence, Maikarfi Metal Construction Ltd has grown to become a leader in engineering, fabrication, and construction. We serve diverse industries with innovative solutions and unmatched quality, striving to exceed client expectations every step of the way.

Our Mission

To deliver world-class engineering and fabrication services tailored to our clients' needs, while fostering sustainable growth and contributing to community development. At Maikarfi Metal Construction Ltd, every project is a testament to our passion for innovation and quality.

What We Do

At Maikarfi Metal Construction Ltd, we specialize in a wide range of services, including:

  • Fabrication: Custom steel poles, hospital and school beds, and water kiosks.
  • Construction: Installation of corrugated overhead tanks, surface tanks, and footings for pedestrian bridges.
  • Renovation: Replacement of timber trusses with steel trusses in hospitals and schools.
  • Specialized Projects: Solar submersible pump conversion and laser cutting of intricate designs.

Our cutting-edge facilities, equipped with state-of-the-art machinery like lathe machines, laser cutters, and plate rollers, enable us to deliver precision and excellence in every project.

Our Achievememnt

Over the years, we have successfully completed projects like:

  • Installation of 125,000-liter and 400,000-liter tanks in Borno State, Nigeria.
  • Construction of container offices for major institutions.
  • Rehabilitation and conversion of high-capacity water systems to solar-powered solutions.
